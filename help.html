● Downloading

To Get MaineCoon head on over to the Download Page and download the
appropriate version for your computer. Next run Mainecoone, and wallah you now
can use Mainecoon for any script execution you want!

● General Usage

So one you've gotten it downloaded and launched you should
be greeted with the Mainecoon GUI, if not then your OS is probably not supported and you will sadly have to wait till it is released on your platform.
Doing the rest is pretty easy first press "Attach" to inject Mainecoon into roblox.
To load a script simply hit "Open File" and navigate
to your script and select it, you can also paste your script into the script box in the GUI
after that you simply hit execute and your script should now be executed into your current session of roblox.
And it's that easy and you are ready to go!
